Objectives

Staff strength and roles

Supplier structure (major and minor suppliers)

License renewal cycle

Expired medicine reimbursement practices

Pricing and discount strategy

Dashboard Highlights

Revenue and order channel visualization

Profit margin comparison between medicines and cosmetics

Customer rating matrix with overall score

Competitive benchmarking charts

Radar and bar charts for service vs price positioning

All insights are consolidated into a single, easy-to-interpret Excel dashboard.

Key Insights

Medicines contribute the majority of revenue, while cosmetics offer slightly higher margins

Strong customer trust and service orientation differentiate Kamalika Kemists

Online and call-based orders form a significant share of sales

Competitors focus more on discounts and diversification, while Kamalika Kemists competes on relationships and delivery

High customer satisfaction reflects operational consistency and staff knowledge
